Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish with a crispy dough base topped with tomato sauce and cheese.

It can be customized with endless toppings to suit any taste.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Basil leaves

Steps

  1. Prepare and stretch the pizza dough.
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Add mozzarella cheese and toppings.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake at 220°C for 15 minutes.
