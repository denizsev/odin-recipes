Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a hearty Italian pasta dish with a rich and meaty tomato sauce.

It is one of the most popular pasta dishes enjoyed all around the world.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion and garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Saute onion and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add ground beef and brown it.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti with parmesan.
