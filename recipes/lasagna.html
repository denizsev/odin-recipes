Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy bechamel.

It is baked in the oven until golden and bubbly, perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Garlic and onion

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles.
  2. Brown the ground beef with garlic and onion.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and bechamel in a baking dish.
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake at 180°C for 45 minutes.
